Case Retrieval and Reuse

Based on the historical case library and global design knowledge base, quickly retrieve similar design solutions and provide reusable design ideas.

Innovative Design Generation

Workflow

Our system follows a scientific and rigorous design process, providing professional support at every step from requirements to solutions.

Requirement Collection and Analysis

Designers collect, organize, and analyze customer requirement information to clarify various requirements such as product functions, performance, cost budgets, etc. AI assists in extracting key information and prioritizing requirements.

1

Solution Generation

The AI agent receives requirement information, uses pre - trained knowledge and database retrieval to generate solutions including historical reusable cases, global reference cases, and detailed design suggestions.

2

Solution Evaluation

Designers evaluate the generated solutions from multiple dimensions, including technical feasibility, cost - effectiveness, operational convenience, etc. AI provides quantitative evaluation indicators and suggestions.

3

Solution Optimization

Based on the evaluation results and user feedback, the AI refines the design solution iteratively to generate an improved solution. This process can be repeated until a satisfactory design is obtained.
